Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RemoCoder (the "App") handles personal information.

Information We Collect

The App uses the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes for connection setup. Images captured by the camera are never transmitted externally.

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information with third parties.

Communications

The App communicates only with the user's own devices via Tailscale VPN. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools or advertising services.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the GitHub repository.
